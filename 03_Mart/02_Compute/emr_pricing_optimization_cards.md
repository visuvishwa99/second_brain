Cloze
In Amazon EMR, the {management fee} is a fixed cost added on top of the base EC2 price.
END

START
Cloze
When using On-Demand instances in EMR, the management fee is roughly {20-25}% of the total cost.
END

START
Cloze
When using Spot instances (with ~90% EC2 discount), the EMR management fee can rise to roughly {50}% of the total bill.
END

START
Cloze
The "EMR Tax" paradox states that as you optimize {compute (EC2)} costs using Spot or RIs, the relative cost of the {EMR management layer} increases significantly.
END

START
Cloze
To avoid the EMR management fee at scale, many organizations migrate to running Spark on {Kubernetes (EKS)}.
END
